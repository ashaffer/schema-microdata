Little Monkey

from Dalkhafine 2 years ago Not Yet Rated

Un petit singe, initié par son maître, va accomplir son passage à l'âge adulte. Il va devoir combattre le feu, métaphore de sa peur de grandir, en apprivoisant le son du djembé sacré, symbole de son indépendance.

Film de fin d'étude réalisé à l'EMCA Angoulême, 2011.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A young monkey, initiated by his master, will pass his transition to adulthood. He will have to fight fire, metaphor for his fear of growing up, by taming the sound of the sacred djembe, symbol of independence.

My graduation film, directed during my last year at EMCA Angoulême, 2011.
